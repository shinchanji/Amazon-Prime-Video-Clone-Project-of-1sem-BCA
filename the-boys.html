The Boys

8.7/10 2019 - Present TV-MA 3 Seasons

A group of vigilantes set out to take down corrupt superheroes who abuse their superpowers. In a world where superheroes embrace the darker side of their massive celebrity and fame, The Boys centers on a group of vigilantes known informally as "The Boys," who set out to take down corrupt superheroes with no more than their blue-collar grit and a willingness to fight dirty.

Genre: Action, Comedy, Crime
